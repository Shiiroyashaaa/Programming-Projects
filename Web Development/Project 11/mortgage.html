Enter the amount of the mortgage, change the yearly interests rate in percentage (e.g., 8.5% = 8.5) if you are quoted a different rate, and select Length of loan in number of years. Click the "calculate" button to view the monthly payment. Click the "Reset" button to clear the form. For person and business loans there will be a $1000.00 and $500.00 origination fees that will be included in monthly payments. There is no origination fees for education loans.
